Learn Microsoft PowerPoint 2016

Microsoft PowerPoint is a presentation program developed by Microsoft. It is a part of the Microsoft Office suite of productivity software, and it is widely used for creating slide-based presentations for various purposes such as business meetings, academic lectures, and more. Below are some resources to help you learn Microsoft PowerPoint 2016:

Step 1: Open Microsoft Powerpoint 2016 and select suitable theme for your presentation

Step 2: In this home tab, you can adjust font, align, sort, borders

Step 3: Can insert table, pictures, shapes using insert tab

Step 4:In design tab you can set the design, slide size and background

Step 5: Can do different transitions for the slides of the presentetion

Step 6: Can do different animations for the contents in the slides

Step 7: In slide show tab there are options to adjust slide show time , custom slide show, record slide show etc
